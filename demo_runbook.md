# Regrowth Demo Builder - Sales Team Runbook

## Overview

The Regrowth Demo Builder allows sales team members to create professional, shareable demo websites for prospects in minutes. Demos automatically expire after 3 days to maintain freshness and security.

## Getting Started

### 1. Access the Platform

- Navigate to: `https://regrowthdemos.com/login`
- Use your Regrowth email and password
- You'll be redirected to the main dashboard

### 2. Creating Your First Demo

1. **Click "Create Demo"** from the dashboard
2. **Fill out the form:**
   - **Business Type**: Select from gym, restaurant, cafe, jewelry store, or salon
   - **Business Name**: Enter the prospect's business name
   - **Tagline**: Optional catchy phrase (e.g., "Transform Your Body")
   - **Pages**: Choose 1-6 pages (3 is recommended)
   - **Color**: Pick a color that matches their brand
   - **Logo**: Upload their logo if available

3. **Preview**: Use the preview panel to see how it looks
4. **Generate**: Click "Generate Demo" to create the website
5. **Share**: Copy the generated link and send to your prospect

## Best Practices

### Choosing the Right Template

- **Gym**: Use for fitness centers, personal trainers, yoga studios
- **Restaurant**: Perfect for fine dining, casual restaurants, catering
- **Cafe**: Ideal for coffee shops, bakeries, casual dining
- **Jewelry**: Great for jewelry stores, watch shops, luxury goods
- **Salon**: Perfect for hair salons, spas, beauty services

### Customization Tips

- **Business Name**: Use the exact name from their website/business card
- **Tagline**: Keep it short and impactful (5-8 words max)
- **Color**: Match their brand colors if known, or use professional defaults
- **Logo**: Always upload if available - it makes demos look more professional

### Sharing Demos

- **Timing**: Share demos within 24 hours of creation
- **Context**: Always explain what the demo shows
- **Follow-up**: Mention the 3-day expiry to create urgency
- **Personalization**: Reference specific features relevant to their business

## Demo Management

### Dashboard Features

- **Active Demos**: See all your created demos
- **View Counts**: Track how many times each demo was viewed
- **Status**: Monitor which demos are still active
- **Quick Actions**: Copy links, view demos, or create new ones

### Demo Lifecycle

1. **Created**: Demo is live and shareable
2. **Active**: Available for 3 days from creation
3. **Expired**: Automatically deleted after 3 days
4. **Views**: Tracked for analytics and follow-up

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Demo not loading:**
- Check if the demo has expired (3-day limit)
- Verify the demo ID is correct
- Try refreshing the page

**Images not showing:**
- This is normal - stock images load from Unsplash
- If images fail, placeholder text will show instead

**Can't create demo:**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page

### Getting Help

- **Technical Issues**: Contact IT support
- **Account Problems**: Contact your manager
- **Feature Requests**: Submit through the feedback form

## Sales Process Integration

### Discovery Phase

1. **Qualify** the prospect's business type
2. **Gather** basic information (name, industry, needs)
3. **Create** demo during the call or immediately after
4. **Share** link and explain what they'll see

### Demo Presentation

1. **Screen share** while showing the demo
2. **Highlight** relevant features for their business
3. **Explain** how Regrowth can customize this for them
4. **Schedule** follow-up based on their interest

### Follow-up Strategy

- **Day 1**: Send demo link with context
- **Day 2**: Check if they viewed it, answer questions
- **Day 3**: Final follow-up before expiry
- **After expiry**: Create new demo if still interested

## Security & Compliance

### Data Protection

- **No Personal Data**: Demos don't store customer information
- **Auto-Expiry**: All demos delete after 3 days
- **View-Only**: Demos can't be modified by viewers
- **Secure Links**: Demo URLs are not guessable

### Best Practices

- **Don't Share**: Login credentials with others
- **Log Out**: When finished using the platform
- **Report Issues**: Any security concerns immediately
- **Follow Policy**: Company guidelines for prospect data

## Tips for Success

### Creating Compelling Demos

1. **Research First**: Look at their current website
2. **Match Style**: Use colors and tone similar to their brand
3. **Highlight Benefits**: Focus on features that solve their problems
4. **Keep It Simple**: Don't over-customize - let them imagine possibilities

### Effective Follow-up

1. **Timing**: Follow up within 24 hours of sending
2. **Value**: Explain how Regrowth can help their specific business
3. **Urgency**: Mention the 3-day demo expiry
4. **Next Steps**: Always have a clear call-to-action
